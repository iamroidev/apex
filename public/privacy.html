Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information required to host and run real-time video sessions:

  • Account Details: When you register or sign in with Google, we save your Google profile name, email, and ID token payload to associate with your classroom workspace.
  • Meeting Logs: We store basic session attendance logs (joining/leaving timestamps and student names) to help instructors track attendance.
  • Media Feeds: Your microphone, camera, and screen share data are processed in real-time by our LiveKit Selective Forwarding Unit (SFU). This media is never recorded or stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information strictly to provide video conferencing and classroom features:

  • To authenticate your identity and show your display name/avatar.
  • To provide real-time chat messaging, interactive polls, and whiteboard sharing.
  • To generate attendance reports for hosts.

3. Third-Party Integrations

We integrate with Google Identity Services for user sign-in. Your Google data is strictly governed by the Google Privacy Policy. We do not share your information with other third-party services or advertising platforms.

4. Data Storage & Security

All database records (passwords, usernames, and whiteboard records) are securely stored on our private SQLite database in AWS. We employ transport layer security (HTTPS/SSL) to protect all data in transit.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at support@apexclassroom.duckdns.org.
